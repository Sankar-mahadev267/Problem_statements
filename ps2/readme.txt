2. Problem Statement: Sum of a Two-Digit number and its reverse

You are tasked with Creating a Program that takes a two digit positive integer as input, Calculates its reverse and Computes sum of original number & reverse. The goal is to ensure that the Program correctly calculates and displays the sum

Input: The Program should accept a two digit Positive integer, n, where 10<=n<=99.

Output: The Program should display sum of two digit original number, and its reverse
